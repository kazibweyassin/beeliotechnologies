## SENDING TIPS

### Best Times to Send:
- **Tuesday-Thursday**: 9-11am or 2-4pm
- Avoid Mondays (inbox overload) and Fridays (weekend mode)

### Personalization Checklist:
âœ“ Use their first name
âœ“ Mention their company specifically
âœ“ Reference something specific about them (LinkedIn post, news, their website)
âœ“ Keep it under 150 words
âœ“ ONE clear call-to-action

### Subject Line Rules:
- Keep under 50 characters
- Ask questions (higher open rates)
- Be specific, not generic
- Avoid spam words: "FREE", "GUARANTEED", "ACT NOW"

### Response Rate Expectations:
- Cold emails: 1-5% response rate
- Send 20-30 per day
- Expect 1-2 replies per day
- 1 meeting per 10 replies
- 1 client per 4-5 meetings

**Goal: 100 emails = 3-5 responses = 1 client**
